# Trust in Public-Facing Scientific Visualizations

Welcome to our study! We are researchers studying how people perceive and trust scientific visualizations that appear in news articles, government reports, and social media.

In this study, you will:

1. View several scientific visualizations on topics like climate change, public health, and energy data.
2. **Think aloud** — speak your thoughts out loud as you examine each visualization. We will record your audio.
3. Answer a few multiple-choice questions about each visualization.
4. Complete a short post-study questionnaire.

The study should take approximately **15–20 minutes**. Your responses are anonymous and will only be used for research purposes.

**Please ensure you are in a quiet environment and have a working microphone before proceeding.**
