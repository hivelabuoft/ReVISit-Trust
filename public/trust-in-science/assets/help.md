# Help

This study asks you to examine scientific visualizations and share your thoughts.

- **Think-aloud**: When viewing each visualization, please speak your thoughts out loud. Describe what you see, what you understand, and how much you trust the information presented.
- **Questions**: After each visualization, answer the multiple-choice questions. Select the best answer for each question.
- **Navigation**: Click **Next** to move to the next section. You cannot go back to previous sections.

If you have technical issues, please contact the research team at the email listed in the sidebar.
